Time of year

when we'll go

Weather and prices swing a lot across the year — here's the honest rundown before you pick.

Roughly: Nov–Feb is cool, dry, and busiest (highest prices). Mar–May is hot and drier with fewer crowds. Jun–Oct is rainy season — showers most days but warm, green, and noticeably cheaper.
